Trump likely can’t touch permitted offshore wind projects in New England

  • Updated: Jan. 31, 2025, 5:50 a.m.
  • | Published: Jan. 31, 2025, 5:50 a.m.
By
  • Hadley Barndollar | HBarndollar@masslive.com

President Donald Trump’s cessation of new offshore wind projects as one of his first matters of business sent a chill throughout the multi-billion dollar industry that’s taken root across the nation.

Advocates in New England, however, are confident the region’s fully-permitted wind projects are likely untouchable to the Trump administration, such as Vineyard Wind 1, Revolution Wind and New England Wind 1 and 2.
